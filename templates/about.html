About Our Group Home

We are a dedicated team providing a secure, nurturing, and growth-oriented home for young adults facing life challenges. Our mission is to empower residents with tools for success and independence.

Our Purpose

  • ✓ Daily living and personal care assistance
  • ✓ Recovery and therapeutic counseling
  • ✓ Skill building: budgeting, cooking, job search
  • ✓ School reintegration and community connection

Key Characteristics

  • ✓ Small group family-style setting
  • ✓ 24/7 supervision and safety
  • ✓ Personalized care plans
  • ✓ Diverse and inclusive environment

Who We Serve

  • ✓ Young adults with disabilities
  • ✓ Individuals with behavioral health needs
  • ✓ Youth exiting foster care or juvenile justice systems

Person-Centered Living

At Loyalty Group Homes, our philosophy is built around three core values that guide how we care for and empower every resident.

Support

Individualized care for each resident, with small community sizes to ensure everyone’s needs are met daily.

Independence

Opportunities to grow, build life skills, and develop confidence in a safe, nurturing environment.

Peace of Mind

A welcoming home that offers safety, dignity, and reassurance to both residents and their families.

If you know someone who may benefit from our supportive living community, contact us today to learn more.
